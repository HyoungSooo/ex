C&C Lab

The Environmental Restoration Lab aims to cultivate competent environmental and energy engineers who can actively cope with the community by acquiring environmental and energy-related expertise to solve the increasingly serious environmental pollution problem in the 21st century. In particular, it uses expertise such as hydrogen energy, waste resources, biomass, and geothermal as a way to secure eco-friendly renewable energy and education to actively and effectively respond to climate change and greenhouse gas reduction as a result.
